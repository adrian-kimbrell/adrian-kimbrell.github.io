About Me

I am a full-stack developer with a passion for building robust and scalable web applications. With a strong background in programming languages like Python, Java, Ruby, and JavaScript, I have experience in web development using HTML, CSS, and frameworks like Flask, Django, and React.

In my previous role at Kaleidoscope, I led the development of a new SaaS product, improving user experience and reducing page render times by 50%. I also have experience with REST API development, database management using MySQL and Redis, and integrating AI models into applications.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Eckerd College and have a strong understanding of Agile methodologies like Scrum and Kanban. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and stay up-to-date with industry trends.
